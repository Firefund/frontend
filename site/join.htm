About the campaign

We are very lucky to be working with the rent strikers in London, setting up a crowdfund
to support the next heat of their campaign in the fall.

They aim to succeed even more than they already have, and are hoping for the strike to spread all over the UK, Europe and everywhere else, where evil landlords are profiting on poor students (and workers).

Raising £5.000 they can cover all expenses for mobilising, spreading and acting - for an entire year!

Help us help the #RentStrike and be prepare to see the UK get lit!

How to support

Basically, the campaign needs all the help it can get.
The rentstrikers are trying to raise minimum £5.000 in 30 days.

This means that the more attention, support, shoutouts, vouching etc. it gets - the better everything looks for the housing situation in the UK.

If you are wondering how you as an individual can support the campaign, we have listed a couple of ideas below.

If you are serious about supporting and want to take part of something bigger - scroll down to the contact section.

What can you do:

  • Donate

  • Follow the activities on #rentstrike on social media and share the shit out of the campaign.

  • Use your network - invite other groups and supporters to spread the word, donate and raise money.

  • Throw a support party or another fundraising event to raise funds for the campaign.

  • Give tips on where to find that big pile of money.

  • Get in contact and coordinate with the activists themselves.
